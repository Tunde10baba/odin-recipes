Pancake Recipe

Credit to: allrecipe website @ Allrecipes

Description

Greek yogurt adds a tangy flavor and soft texture to pancakes. Give this recipe a go and have fluffy pancakes on the table in just 15 minutes! Serve the pancakes warm with any leftover yogurt and a fruit compote for a special brunch dish.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces Greek yogurt
  • 1 large egg
  • half a cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Whisk yogurt in a medium bowl until smooth and creamy. Add egg and whisk to combine
  2. Combine flour and baking soda in a small bowl; add to yogurt mixture and whisk until batter is smooth
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the hot griddle and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes
  5. Repeat with remaining batter
