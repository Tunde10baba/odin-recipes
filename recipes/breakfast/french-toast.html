French Toast Recipe

Credit to: allrecipe website @ Allrecipes

Description

This sheet pan pumpkin spice French toast is sweetened with flavored coffee creamer. For extra fall flavor, top off this breakfast dish with pumpkin butter

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup pumpkin spice coffee creamer
  • 1/3 cup canned pumpkin
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup, plus more for serving
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (16 ounce) loaf French bread, cut into 1-inch slices
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice or ground cinnamon
  • pumpkin butter, sliced almonds, and/or powdered sugar, for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Coat a 10x15-inch rimmed sheet pan with cooking spray or line with parchment paper
  2. Whisk together eggs, creamer, pumpkin, maple syrup, and salt in a bowl; pour into a shallow dish. Soak bread slices in egg mixture, turning once, until well soaked. Shingle bread slices in the prepared pan; sprinkle with pumpkin pie spice
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes. Serve with pumpkin butter, almonds, powdered sugar, and/or additional maple syrup
